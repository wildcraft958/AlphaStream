Title: 124,446 Shares in Alphabet Inc. $GOOG Bought by First Horizon Corp
Source: MarketBeat
Date: 20260119T090252

First Horizon Corp has acquired 124,446 shares of Alphabet Inc. (GOOG), establishing it as their 11th largest holding, valued at approximately $30.3 million.
