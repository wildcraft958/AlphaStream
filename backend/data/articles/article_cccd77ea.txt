Title: Insider Selling: Alphabet (NASDAQ:GOOGL) Director Sells 600 Shares of Stock
Source: MarketBeat
Date: 20260116T022322
---
Alphabet Director John Hennessy sold 600 shares of GOOGL stock for $202,512 on January 13th, reducing his stake by 2.75%. Despite the insider selling, Alphabet's stock traded down 0.9% to $332.78, with a market capitalization of $4.02 trillion, and the company recently surpassed quarterly EPS and revenue expectations. Near-term catalysts for Alphabet include strong AI momentum and device integrations for Gemini, though risks such as copyright litigation, data-center transmission constraints, and regulatory pressures persist.
