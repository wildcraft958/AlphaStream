Title: Gene Munster Says Apple's Move From OpenAI's ChatGPT To Google Gemini Significantly Raises Chances Of New Siri Succeeding: 'Nice Job'
Source: Yahoo Finance
Date: 20260115T213100
---
Gene Munster, managing partner at Deepwater Asset Management, believes Apple's decision to switch from OpenAI's ChatGPT to Google Gemini for its new Siri significantly increases the chances of Siri's success. He praised Apple CEO Tim Cook for addressing Siri's long-standing weaknesses and leveraging the partnership for future growth in devices and services. This move signals a more serious approach from Apple towards enhancing Apple Intelligence, according to Munster.
