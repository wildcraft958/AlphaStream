Title: After Apple Deal, Is Google — Not OpenAI — Now the Premier AI Stock?
Source: Barchart.com
Date: 20260114T195656
---
Google's new partnership with Apple, integrating its Gemini 3 AI model into iPhones, is poised to reshape the AI competitive landscape. This deal strengthens Google's position in search and cloud services, propelling it as a premier AI stock over rivals like OpenAI. Analysts maintain a "Strong Buy" consensus for Alphabet stock, citing its AI-driven trajectory and potential for significant market share gains.
