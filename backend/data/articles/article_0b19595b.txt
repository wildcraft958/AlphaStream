Title: Alphabet Inc. (NASDAQ:GOOGL) Receives Consensus Recommendation of "Moderate Buy" from Analysts
Source: MarketBeat
Date: 20260115T064148

Alphabet Inc. (NASDAQ:GOOGL) has received a "Moderate Buy" consensus recommendation from 52 analysts, with an average 12-month price target of $326.31, driven by AI momentum and strategic wins like Apple choosing Gemini for Siri. The company also announced a quarterly dividend of $0.21 per share.
