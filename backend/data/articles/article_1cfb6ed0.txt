Title: Motco Lowers Holdings in Alphabet Inc. $GOOGL
Source: MarketBeat
Date: 20260115T095713
---
Motco reduced its stake in Alphabet Inc. (NASDAQ:GOOGL) by 2.8% in Q3, selling 3,197 shares to hold 111,392 shares valued at approximately $27.08 million, making it their fourth largest holding. This comes amidst significant insider selling by CEO Sundar Pichai and others, though analysts widely maintain a "Moderate Buy" rating with a consensus target price of $326.31. Alphabet recently surpassed a $4 trillion market capitalization, buoyed by accelerating AI commercialization and strategic wins like Apple's selection of Gemini.
