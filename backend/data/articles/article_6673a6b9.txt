Title: CORRECTION -- Adams Diversified Equity Fund Announces 2025 Performance
Source: GlobeNewswire
Date: 20260115T193100
---
Adams Diversified Equity Fund, Inc. (NYSE: ADX) issued a corrected release regarding its 2025 performance, specifically amending a typographical error from 2.4% to 2.7% in the third paragraph. The fund reported an 18.9% total return on net asset value for 2025, outperforming the S&P 500 Index and the Morningstar U.S. Large Blend Category. The correction highlights the fund's strong performance relative to its benchmarks and peers.
